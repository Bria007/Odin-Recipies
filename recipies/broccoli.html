The Broccoli with Garlic Butter and Cashews-The Recipie We All Need!

This brocoli recipie is the one you've been looking for. It's perfect for getting the kids and the hubby to love eating green food<3

So now you're thinking, well arent all brocolli recipies created equal? The answer is no! The spices in this recipie will show you what makes this recipie so different.

Getting Started

Ingredients

      1 ½ pounds fresh broccoli, cut into bite size pieces
      ⅓ cup butter
      1 tablespoon brown sugar
      3 tablespoons soy sauce
      2 teaspoons white vinegar
      ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
      2 cloves garlic, minced
      ⅓ cup chopped salted cashews

Directions

  1. Place the broccoli into a large pot with about 1 inch of water in the bottom. Bring to a boil, and cook for 7 minutes, or until tender but still crisp. Drain, and arrange broccoli on a serving platter.
  2. While the broccoli is cooking, melt the butter in a small skillet over medium heat. Mix in the brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, pepper and garlic. Bring to a boil, then remove from the heat. Mix in the cashews, and pour the sauce over the broccoli. Serve immediately.
